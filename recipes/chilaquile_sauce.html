Red Chilaquile Sauce

Description

This is a red chilaquile sauce that can be added to fried corn tortillas to make a delicious red chilaquiles. Top with egg or add shredded chicken if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chile de arbol
  • 1 Chile guajillo
  • 3-4 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 serrano pepper or jalapeno
  • 1/2 white onion
