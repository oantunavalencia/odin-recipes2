Red Chilaquile Sauce

Description

This is a red chilaquile sauce that can be added to fried corn tortillas to make a delicious red chilaquiles. Top with egg or add shredded chicken if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chile de arbol
  • 1 Chile guajillo
  • 3-4 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 serrano pepper or jalapeno pepper
  • 1/2 white onion

Recipe Steps

  1. Cut roma tomatoes, onions, and pepper in half.
  2. Crush clove of garlic.
  3. Broil on high the garlic, pepper, tomatoes, and onion. Remove from broiler once they begin to brown.
  4. Deseed chiles de arbol and guajillo chile. Rehydrate in hot water for 2 minutes.
  5. Place all ingredients into a food processor and process until a smooth sauce is formed.
  6. Simmer with oil in a pan.
  7. Store in jar and place in fridge.
